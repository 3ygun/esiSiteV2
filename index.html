Are you a gamer?

ESI is first and foremost an organization of people who are passionate about gaming. We have members who love to play all sorts of casual and competitive games! If you love gaming – and you love the gaming community, this is a great place to meet new people and make new friends!

ESI also focuses on promoting eSports in the collegiate and public scene. We hold competitive and casual tournaments for games like League of Legends, DOTA, Super Smash Brothers, and much more. If you’re into competition, or you just like to play games with your friends, ESI is the place for you!
