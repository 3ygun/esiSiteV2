About ESI

" E-Sports Initiative works to bring together recreational and competitive video game players, as well as the digital media industry, to raise money for local and national causes. We work to beneficially impact the community while maintaining a positive image of the industry and its consumers. More directly, we hold video game events and tournaments to raise funds for charity and we volunteer in the local Columbus community all while improving the Midwest eSports scene. " - ESI Mission Statement
